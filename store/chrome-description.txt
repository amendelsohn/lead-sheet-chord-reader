LeadSheet Chord Reader gives you a clean, distraction-free view of any chord sheet. One click opens a focused overlay right on top of the page — no copying, no switching tabs.

FEATURES
- Transpose up or down to any key
- Auto-scroll at adjustable speed — hands-free practice
- Multiple color themes (Nord, Dracula, Solarized, and more)
- Capo, key, and tuning info displayed in the header
- Keyboard shortcuts for everything

WORKS WITH
- Ultimate Guitar (tabs.ultimate-guitar.com)
- E-Chords (e-chords.com)
- CifraClub (cifraclub.com)
- AZChords (azchords.com)
- UkuTabs (ukutabs.com)
- Chordie (chordie.com)
- More sites coming soon

HOW IT WORKS
Navigate to a chord page on any supported site. A floating button appears in the corner — click it to open the reader. That's it. Your preferences (theme, font size, scroll speed) are saved automatically.

The reader runs entirely in your browser. No data is sent anywhere. No account required.
